Features

The Yamaha R1M is a high-performance sportbike designed for riders seeking the thrill of the track and the open road. With its 998cc Crossplane 4-cylinder engine, the R1M delivers explosive power and torque, providing an exhilarating riding experience at every twist of the throttle.

Specifications:

  • 998cc Crossplane 4-cylinder engine
  • Electronic Racing Suspension (ERS)
  • Quick Shift System (QSS)
  • Yamaha Ride Control (YRC) with multiple riding modes
  • Carbon fiber bodywork for reduced weight
  • Highly aerodynamic design for improved performance
